4. User Roles

CharityConnect supports three roles: User (attendees who browse and purchase tickets), Organiser (verified individuals or groups who create and manage charity events), and Admin (platform administrators). Organiser accounts require verification by an administrator before events can be published.

5. Event Ticketing and Payments

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. CharityConnect does not store your card details. When you purchase a ticket, you will receive a digital receipt with a QR code for verification. Ticket prices and charity allocation percentages are displayed on each event page before purchase.

6. Refund Policy

Refund requests may be submitted within 24 hours of placing an order via the "My Orders" page. After this window has passed, refund requests will no longer be available. All refund requests are reviewed by the event organiser, who may approve or deny them at their discretion. Approved refunds will be processed through the original payment method.

7. Charity Fund Allocation

Organisers assign allocation percentages to verified beneficiary charities for each event. These percentages are displayed publicly on the event page. CharityConnect provides transparency tools including allocation reports and payout summaries, but does not directly handle the transfer of funds to charities.

8. AI-Generated Content

CharityConnect offers optional AI-assisted features to help organisers generate event descriptions and marketing suggestions. AI-generated content is provided as a suggestion only. Organisers are responsible for reviewing and approving all content before publication. CharityConnect does not guarantee the accuracy of AI-generated text.
